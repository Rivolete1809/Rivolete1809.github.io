Eclipse Dining (日食餐厅)

Where Light Meets Shadow, and Flavor Becomes Art

At Eclipse Dining, we design a culinary experience that unites monochromatic elegance with the rich cultural heritage of Eastern and Western culinary traditions. Inspired by the powerful visual effect of a solar eclipse, our design and cuisine aim to achieve a perfect balance of contrast, harmony, and creative expression. Driven by a passionate desire for culinary innovation and storytelling by means of flavour, our kitchen crew carefully crafts a menu that features exceptional ingredients, captivating flavour, and visually appealing designs. From a sophisticated Black & White Truffle Carbonara to a dramatic Zebra Wellington, paired with a beguiling Eclipse Phantom Cocktail, each dish is designed to be as captivating as the phenomenon in the skies that inspired it. Enter Eclipse Dining, a restaurant whose each dining experience embodies the harmony of light and darkness, tradition and contemporary, and taste and elegance.
